Demo canvas #8

Numerical solutions to the pendulum equation by 3blue1brown: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p_di4Zn4wz4 (14:28).

Position the starting points by dragging and click “animate” to see the solutions (2,000 iterations). The iteration step is 0.005 by default, and can be adjusted in your browser’s console by changing variable step. The length and the dampening coefficient can be changed with the sliders underneath the canvas.

The axes are theta and theta-dot (I apologize, it’s not possible to label them with vtvt yet). This demo runs a bit “heavy” on older mobile devices.
